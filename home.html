In Pursuit Of The Fourth Dimension

A cognitive approach to the visualization of four orthogonal spatial dimensions. This technology aims to expand the mind to intuit and navigate four dimensional space.

If successful this groundbreaking technology answers questions like "What does a four dimensional cube really look like?" and "Can the human mind imagine four orthogonal directions?" apart from eventually opening up the world of four dimensional problem solving and even the four dimensional metaverse.

Studying the brain as it begins to understand four dimensions is an at least equally exciting prospect as understanding 4D itself.
