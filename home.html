In Pursuit OfThe Fourth Dimension

Based on a cognitive approach to the visualization of four spatial dimensions, this technology aims to expand the mind and enable it to intuit and navigate four dimensional space.

If successful this groundbreaking technology answers questions like "What does a four dimensional cube really look like?" and "Can the human mind imagine four orthogonal directions?" apart from eventually opening up the world of four dimensional problem solving and even a four dimensional metaverse.

Studying the brain as it begins to understand four dimensions is a prospect at least as exciting as understanding 4D itself.

Math

Rotation Math

R = A tiny rotation, I = Identity matrix, m = Infinitesmal matrix, v = any vector

  1. R = I + m
  2. ((I + m) v)2 = v2 (Because it's a rigid body transformation)
  3. vT(I + mT)(I + m) v = vTv (Rewrite in matrix form)
  4. vT(I + mT + m) v = vTv (Drop m2 terms)
  5. vT(mT + m) v = 0
  6. mT + m = 0
  7. therefore m must be antisymmetric
